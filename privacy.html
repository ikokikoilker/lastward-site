Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

In short: Lastward has no accounts and no sign-up. Your progress is saved on your device. We never sell your data and we never track you across other apps. The game shows optional, non-personalized rewarded ads only when you choose to watch them, and collects anonymous usage and crash statistics to improve the game.

1. Who we are

Lastward is developed and published by an independent solo developer ("we", "us"). If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at the address in section 9.

2. Data stored on your device

Your game progress, unlocks, settings, and run saves are stored locally on your device. We have no server and no access to this data. You can erase it at any time via Settings → Reset Progress inside the game, or by deleting the app.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage events — for example that a run started or ended, how long it lasted, which language the game is set to, and whether a purchase was completed. This data is aggregated, is not used to identify you, and is used solely to improve the game.

4. Crash reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics (Google) to receive crash reports (device model, OS version, and a technical trace of the crash) so we can find and fix bugs.

5. Advertising

Lastward contains optional rewarded video ads only — shown exclusively when you choose to watch one for an in-game reward. There are no banner ads and no forced ads. Ads are served by Google AdMob and are configured as non-personalized: we do not request tracking permission (App Tracking Transparency) and we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites. The ad provider may process basic technical data (such as IP address and device model) to serve ads and prevent fraud. If you purchase the Supporter Pack, you can claim rewarded-ad rewards without watching ads.

6. In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.

7. Game Center

If you enable Game Center, leaderboards and achievements are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy.

8. What we don't do

  • No accounts, no registration, no collection of your name, email, contacts, photos, or location.
  • No selling or renting of data to anyone.
  • No personalized advertising profiles, no cross-app tracking.

9. Third-party services

  • Google AdMob & Firebase — Google Privacy Policy, Firebase Privacy
  • Apple (purchases, Game Center) — Apple Privacy Policy

10. Children

Lastward is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are always non-personalized for all players.

11. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR, CCPA, and Türkiye's KVKK), you may have rights to access or request deletion of personal data. Since we do not collect data that identifies you, in most cases there is nothing for us to delete — analytics data is anonymous, and local game data can be erased from within the game. For any request or question, contact us and we will help.

12. Changes

If we change this policy (for example when adding a new feature), we will update this page and its effective date.
