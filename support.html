Support

Answers to common questions about Lastward. Can't find yours? Contact us below — we read every message.

How is my progress saved?

Everything is saved locally on your device — no account needed. If you delete the app, local progress is deleted with it.

How do I restore my Supporter Pack purchase?

Open Settings inside the game and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the purchase.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings → Reset Progress. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

How do I change the language?

Open Settings and pick your language. Lastward is fully localized — menus, stories, and all.

An ad reward didn't arrive / an ad won't load

Rewarded ads need an internet connection and available ad inventory. Check your connection and try again in a few minutes. If a reward was lost, email us and we'll make it right.

I found a bug

Please email us with your device model, iOS version, and what happened — a screenshot or screen recording helps a lot.
